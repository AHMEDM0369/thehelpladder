Our Campaign!
“We are powered by a belief that a better world – free from hunger, poverty and illiteracy is possible with a strong aim to guide and serve the needy.” Every person has the right to access resources and opportunities in order to live and develop with dignity and to become an active and contributing member of society.


The Help Ladder Foundation (THLF) has its headquarters in the Basavakalyan District of Bidar, North Karnataka, India. It is one of the most powerful youth volunteer crowdfunding organisations.


Our Mission

On November 11, 2020, THLF was founded with the purpose of advancing a particular cause and restoring hope for a better future. THLF will make every effort to spark change by being active and innovative and by reflecting transparency in all of its operations.

Growing economic inequality, a global pandemic, and the climatic disaster have all had an impact on people all around the world. As a result, THLF was able to provide young people the power to change the future and assist communities in need of money to get through a significant crisis and face a slew of obstacles.
